Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Discount Fence Operations ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Discount Fence Hub application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our fence operations management services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and role within the organization
  • Business Data: Project details, estimates, invoices, and customer information entered into the system
  • QuickBooks Integration: When connected, we access QuickBooks data (customers, invoices, classes) to synchronize business operations

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide fence operations management services
  • Generate quotes, estimates, and invoices
  • Synchronize data with QuickBooks Online
  • Improve our services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • QuickBooks Online: For accounting integration (with your authorization)
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase, Netlify) necessary to operate the application

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure token storage for third-party integrations
  • Role-based access controls

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

7. QuickBooks Integration

When you connect QuickBooks Online, we access your accounting data solely to provide synchronization services. You can disconnect QuickBooks at any time through the application settings, which will revoke our access.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact Discount Fence at the email address associated with your account.
